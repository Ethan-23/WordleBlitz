# WordleBlitz

## Play Here: https://ethan-23.github.io/WordleBlitz/

### How to play
This is a fun spin-off of the game Wordle. You have 30 seconds to figure out the 5-letter word. The sooner you guess, the more points you score. As you go through the rounds, you may not repeat the same 5-letter words unless the word is the answer. If the time runs out or you do not guess it by the final guess, you will lose a life.
